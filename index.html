About This Website ▾

Welcome to my portfolio! I built this space to be a clear, living snapshot of my work and how I think. A site like this carries more context than a PDF resume, as it shows personality, provides room for detail, and evolves with new experience. It’s also a place to share projects and the tools I use every day. Under the hood it’s simple: HTML and CSS with a touch of JavaScript for tasteful animations and accessibility, hosted on GitHub Pages. The goal is clean, fast, and easy to maintain, so the content does the talking.

About My Professional Career ▾

I'm a technology enthusiast, problem solver, and people-first IT professional with over six years of hands-on experience focused on IT operations, infrastructure, and service management. I earned two Associate degrees from Volunteer State Community College and hold multiple IT certifications. As college came to a close, I landed my first internship as an IT Field Engineer. Since then, I’ve gained experience in a wide range of environments, from a corporate IT Service Desk to two startups in infrastructure and engineering. Today I serve as a Senior IT Systems Administrator at one of the world’s leading cybersecurity companies.

About My Personal Life ▾

Life isn’t just work; I have a few side quests too! I’m a big fan of sports, gaming, and travel. One of my long-term goals is to visit all 30 MLB stadiums. So far I’ve hit 15, which means I’ve still got a lot of miles (and hot dogs) to go! When it comes to gaming, I was once a hardcore PC gamer though these days I stick to console. Whether you’re here to talk tech or trade ballpark rankings, I’m just as happy to connect personally as I am professionally. You’ll find links to my social profiles on the Home and Connect sections!
